Wheel Chair Using Just A Glove

Hello Friends this is a project that I made for the handicapped people, those who cannot walk and use a wheel chair. They either have to depend on someone else or have to use their own hands to roll the wheels so I think this can ease their lives a bit.

Air Mouse, Mouse in Air

Ever imagined you are sitting in an aeroplane and suddenly the mousepad of your laptop stops working and you have to take out your USB mouse to continue, how uncomfortable you will feel. Thinking about this I built an air-mouse, which has a glove and depending on you hand movement the sensor inside the glove will move the mouse pointer of your laptop.
